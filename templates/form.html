EMPLOYEE ABSENTEEISM PREDICTION

Problem Statement

Employee absenteeism negatively affects productivity. This project predicts whether an employee will be excessively absent during a workday, defined as absence for more than 3 hours. The model uses factors such as: Number of children, Number of pets, Body mass index (BMI), Reasons for absence, Age, Transportation expenses, Education level and Month of the year.

Goal: Predicting absenteeism in advance to enhance workforce planning and reduce productivity losses.
